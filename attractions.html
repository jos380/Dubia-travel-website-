Must-Visit Attractions

Handpicked attractions with quick tips and what to expect.

Burj Khalifa

Visit the At The Top observation decks for panoramic views. Pre-book tickets to avoid queues.

Dubai Marina

Evening promenades, dining and yacht tours. Great for nightlife and sunset photos.

Desert Safari

Dune bashing, camel rides and traditional entertainment at desert camps. Bring sun protection and closed shoes.
