Where to Stay

Curated hotel picks and neighbourhood tips for different traveller styles.

Burj Al Arab — Ultimate Luxury

An iconic sail-shaped hotel on its own island. Lavish suites, private beach access and Michelin-starred dining.

  • Best for: Luxury experience, special occasions
  • Tip: Book a package with dining credits for better value

Atlantis, The Palm — Family & Entertainment

Located on Palm Jumeirah with Aquaventure Waterpark and an impressive aquarium — great for families.

  • Best for: Families, waterpark access
  • Tip: Stay in ocean rooms for direct aquarium views

Jumeirah Beach Hotel — Beachfront Comfort

Perfect for beach lovers; easy access to JBR and water sports.

  • Best for: Beach & leisure
  • Tip: Choose rooms with sea view and early breakfast service

Rove Hotels — Smart Budget

Stylish, budget-friendly chain with modern rooms and central locations.

  • Best for: Budget-conscious travellers
  • Tip: Convenient for short stays and city access
